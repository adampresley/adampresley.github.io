Ice, Ice, Baby

2009.12.09 · general, culture

Yesterday the family and I went out to Grapevine to see the Gaylord Texan ICE! exhibit. With over 2 million pounds of ice in a 14,000 square foot compound ICE was a very cool (literally and figuratively) experience. Large parkas were provided to ward off the 9 degree temperatures at this winter wonderland, but despite the chilly atmosphere and fairly steep price, it was well worth the trouble and the money. A very beautiful exhibit, and a fun time had by all.
